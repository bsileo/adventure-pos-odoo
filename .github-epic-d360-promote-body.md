## Goal

D360 transaction XLSX imports currently land in **staging/archive** models (`adventure.history.order` / `adventure.history.order.line`) via `adventure.d360.history.import.batch` (`addons/adventure_d360_migration`). That is the right place to normalize and review data, but it is **not first-class** for standard Odoo POS, sales, accounting, and reporting.

This epic tracks the **follow-up**: after data is reviewed in staging, **materialize** it into the appropriate **native Odoo documents** so migrated tenants get a seamless experience (lookup, reporting, and integrations that expect core models).

## Context

- Wizard: `adventure.d360.history.import.wizard` → batch → `action_import_archive`.
- Archive orders are explicitly **non-posting** (`adventure.history.order`).
- Idempotency keys already exist: `d360_source_xmlid`, per-line `d360_line_xmlid`.

## Epic success criteria

- Document type and accounting/stock policy are **decided and documented** (including explicit non-goals, e.g. no GL repost for closed periods).
- Promotion is **idempotent** and **traceable** (each archive order/line maps to at most one native artifact; safe re-run).
- Preconditions for promotion (partner/product match, company, location/POS config) are **defined and enforced**.
- Operators have a clear **UX path** (promote batch or selection, dry-run, error summary).
